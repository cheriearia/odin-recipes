Danish Dream Cake

Description

A soft vanilla cake with an easy broiled coconut-caramel icing and a slightly crisp texture that contrasts with the tender cake.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 3/4 cup whole milk
  • 3 large eggs, at room temperature
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 3/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon almond extract
  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder

Topping

  • 5 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 1/4 cup whole milk
  • 1/2 cup firmly packed brown sugar
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 1 pinch ground nutmeg
  • 1 cup unsweetened shredded coconut
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F
  2. Line an 8x8-inch square pan with enough parchment to have overhang on all sides
  3. For cake, place butter and milk in a microwave-safe bowl
  4. Cook, stirring often, until butter is melted and mixture is hot, 2 to 2 1/2 minutes
  5. Add eggs, white sugar, salt, and nutmeg to a large bowl
  6. Beat with an electric mixer on medium-high speed until mixture has lightened in color and tripled in volume, 3 to 5 minutes
  7. Add in vanilla extract and almond extract and beat until combined
  8. Add flour and baking powder and mix until nearly incorporated, but some dry bits of flour still remain
  9. With mixer running on low speed, gradually pour in hot butter-milk mixture and beat until incorporated
  10. Pour batter into the prepared pan
  11. Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center of the cake comes out clean, 35 to 45 minutes
  12. Meanwhile, for topping, place butter, milk, brown sugar, salt, and nutmeg into a high-sided microwave-safe bowl
  13. Cook, stirring after every 1 minute, until sugar is dissolved and mixture is bubbling, 2 to 3 minutes
  14. Remove from the microwave and stir in coconut flakes and vanilla
  15. Remove cake from the oven and preheat the oven's broiler
  16. Carefully pour topping over the hot cake, and gently spread into an even layer, covering the top of the cake
  17. Place cake under the broiler; broil until topping is broiling and slightly browned, 2 to 3 minutes
  18. Remove cake from the oven; cool in the pan for about 15 minutes
  19. Use parchment overhang to remove to a wire rack to cool completely
