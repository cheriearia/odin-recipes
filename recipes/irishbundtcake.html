Irish Cream Bundt Cake

Description

A velevety, tender cake infused with a creamy liqueur to offer a rich flavor profile with a slight boozy kick.

Ingredients

  • Cooking spray with flour
  • 1 cup chopped pecans
  • 1 (15.25 ounce) package yellow cake mix
  • 1 (3.4 ounce) package instant vanilla pudding mix
  • 4 large eggs
  • 3/4 cups Irish cream liqueur
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 1/4 cup water

Glaze

  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1/4 cup Irish cream liqueur

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 325 degrees F.
  2. Grease and flour a 10-inch Bundt pan with cooking spray.
  3. Sprinkle chopped nuts over the bottom of the pan.
  4. Combine cake mix and pudding mix in a large bowl.
  5. Beat in eggs, Irish cream liqueur, oil, and water with an electric mixer on high speed for 5 minutes.
  6. Pour batter over nuts in a prepared pan.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center of cake comes out clean, about 1 hour.
  8. Let cake cool in the pan for 10 minutes.
  9. While cake is cooling, make glaze: combine sugar, butter, and water in a small saucepan.
  10. Bring to a boil and continue boiling for 5 minutes, stirring constantly.
  11. Remove from heat and stir in Irish cream.
  12. Invert cake onto a serving dish. Prick the top and sides of cake with a toothpick.
  13. Spoon glaze over the top and brush onto the sides.
  14. Allow cake to absorb glaze and then repeat until glaze is used up.
