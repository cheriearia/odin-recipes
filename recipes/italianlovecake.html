Italian Love Cake

Description

A classic Italian-American dessert featuring a layer of chocolate cake, a layer of sweetened ricotta cheese filling, and a light chocolate pudding and whipped topping, creating a moist three-layer cake.

Ingredients

  • 1 (15.25) ounce package chocolate cake mix
  • 1 cup water
  • 7 large eggs, divided
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 pints part-skim ricotta cheese
  • 3/4 cup white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 (3.9) ounce package instant chocolate pudding mix
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 (12 ounce) container frozen whipped topping, thawed

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F.
  2. Lightly grease a 9x13-inch baking dish.
  3. Combine cake mix, water, 3 eggs, and oil in a large bowl.
  4. Beat with an electric mixer until combned, about 2 minutes.
  5. Pour batter into prepared baking dish. Set aside.
  6. Combine ricotta cheese, sugar, vanilla, and remaining 4 eggs in a separate bowl; blend well.
  7. Spread ricotta mixture evenly over the top of the cake batter.
  8. Bake in the preheated oven until or toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean, about 1 hour 15 minutes if using a glass baking dish, or 1 1/2 hours if using a metal pan.
  9. Allow cake to cool completely before topping.
  10. Blend pudding mix and milk until thickened.
  11. Blend in whipped topping.
  12. Spread over cooled cake.
